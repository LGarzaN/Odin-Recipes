Protein Shake for bulking

Description

This is the shake i have been eating every morning before going to the gym for the past weeks, and i just love the taste and it's so easy to eat in the morning after waking up

This shake contains a lot of calories, which makes it a great way to start the day for skinny people like me who struggle to eat enough calories every day.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Strawberries
  • Milk
  • Protein Powder
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Oats
  • Banana
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Add 250ml of milk to the blender
  2. Add as many frozen strawberries as you like and one banana
  3. Add one spoonfull of vanilla ice cream and one spoonfull of yogurt
  4. Add one scoop of protein powder and one scoop of oats
  5. OPTIONAL: Add some ice to get better a better consistency
  6. Blend until you get the consistency you like
