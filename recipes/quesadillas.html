Quesadillas

Description

This is also one of my favourite homemade meals, since it is very simple to make and doesn't require much experience nor ingredients to make

These aren't just regular quesadillas, since i like to add ham to give it some extra flavor and protein.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Heat up a frying pan and wait about a minute for it to heat up
  2. Put the tortillas on the pan and wait till they are cooked on one side
  3. Once they are cooked on one side, flip them and add a slice of ham and the cheese
  4. Close the quesadilla and wait for the cheese to melt and for the tortilla to be cooked
  5. remove from the pan and add salsa for extra flavour and enjoy!
