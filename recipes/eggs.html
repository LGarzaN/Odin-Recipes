Scrambled Eggs

Description

Regular scrambled eggs are boring, so i decided to add some more ingredients to add more flavor and to make them more enjoyable, since they are a pretty popular meal.

By adding these ingredients to your scrambled eggs, you can make them much more tasty and since these ingredients are mostly vegetables, you can eat healthy.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Half a tomato
  • half of an onion
  • Jalapeno pepper (optional)
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan and add cooking oil
  2. After the pan is heated up, add the onions and wait 1 minute for them to cook
  3. Add the tomatoes and the eggs and stir
  4. Cook until you like and then remove the food from the pan into a plate
  5. Enjoy!
